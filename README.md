# Walmart Sales Data Analysis & Holiday Impact Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Walmart sales data to identify trends, seasonal patterns, and the impact of holidays on weekly sales.  
The analysis combines **Python-based data analysis** and a **Power BI interactive dashboard** for business insights.
## Objectives
- Analyze weekly sales trends across multiple Walmart stores
- Identify seasonal and monthly sales patterns
- Compare Holiday vs Non-Holiday sales performance
- Study the impact of external factors such as temperature
- Build a professional Power BI dashboard for decision-making

- Total Sales, Average Weekly Sales, Total Stores KPIs
- Sales Trend Over Time (Holiday vs Non-Holiday)
- Monthly & Seasonal Sales Analysis
- Holiday Impact Comparison
- Temperature vs Sales Correlation
- Interactive filters (Year, Month, Holiday Type)
## Key Insights
- Non-Holiday weeks contribute the majority of total revenue
- Holiday weeks show sharp sales spikes during specific months
- Sales peak during November and December
- External factors like temperature show moderate influence on sales
## Conclusion
This project demonstrates how data analysis and visualization can help retailers understand customer demand, optimize inventory, and improve sales forecasting.
